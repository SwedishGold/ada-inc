### 3. Settings
- Turn ON "Collect email addresses" (if available)

### 4. IMPORTANT - Make public
- Click "Share" button
- Under "General access" change to "Anyone with the link"
- This makes it possible for anyone to respond

### 5. Publish
- Click "Publish" button to make it live

### 6. Test it
- Fill out the form yourself
- Check if response appears in "Responses" tab

### 7. Get email notifications
- In the form editor, click on "Responses" tab
- Look for a bell/notification icon
- Click it to enable email notifications for new responses
