# LinkedIn Competitor Research Skill

## Purpose
Research competitors and similar AI companies/people on LinkedIn to learn what they do to get more followers.

## How to Use

### 1. Search for Targets
Search for:
- AI entrepreneurs in Sweden with many followers
- AI tools companies
- MedTech/HealthTech companies in Sweden
- People posting about AI, prompt engineering, consciousness

### 2. Analyze
Look at:
- What types of posts get most engagement?
- Which hashtags do they use?
- How often do they post?
- What's their "hook" to get people to follow?

### 3. Create Report
Create a report in `ada-inc/leads/competitors-linkedin-YYYY-MM-DD.md` with:
- List of 5-10 competitors/inspiration sources
- What they do well
- Concrete tips for Ada Inc.

2. **Hashtags:** #AI, #Innovation, #Sweden, #Startup, #Tech, #AGI
3. **Frequency:** 3-5 posts/week
4. **Content:** Personal stories, practical tips, product demos
