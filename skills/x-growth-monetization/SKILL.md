# X (Twitter) Growth & Monetization Guide

## Requirements for Monetization
- 500+ verified followers
- 5M organic impressions (last 3 months)
- Premium subscription

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Choose Niche & Optimize Profile
- Focus on 1-2 topics: AI, MedTech, entrepreneurship
- Clear bio: "AI that creates | Building Ada Inc."
- Professional profile picture
- Pin strong post, update every 48 hours

### 2. Build Followers Through Interaction
- Follow verified accounts in your niche
- Reply with valuable comments, not just likes
- Engage with viral tweets to get seen
- Join X Spaces and conversations
- Reply to ALL comments on your posts

### 3. Create Engaging Content Consistently
- Post daily: short tweets, threads (7-10 tweets), images, videos
- Use hooks: "The biggest mistake people make..."
- Create "positive controversy" - topics with multiple perspectives
- Use storytelling, personal stories, questions
- Give value first: tips, insights, entertainment
- Post when your audience is active

### 4. Monetize
- X Ads Revenue Sharing (after 5M impressions)
- Creator Subscriptions (exclusive content)
- Affiliate links
- Sponsored posts (need 100k+ followers)
- Digital products (courses, prompts)

## Key Tips
- 100 verified followers per week is achievable
- Authenticity > perfection
- Share failures and wins
- Don't spam or cross-post
- Engage 3-4 hours/day as beginner
